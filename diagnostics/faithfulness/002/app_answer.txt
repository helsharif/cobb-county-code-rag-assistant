The minimum burial depth for underground fire main piping in Cobb County is 48 inches from the top of the pipe to the finished asphalt grade. This depth is specified for asphaltic concrete pavements and includes proper bedding and backfill requirements to ensure compaction and protection of the pipe (Local 10).

No other specific burial depth details for underground fire mains were found in the provided documents or web sources. The 48-inch depth is the clearly stated minimum in the available local ordinance excerpt.

Sources: Cobb County Code of Ordinances (Local 10).
