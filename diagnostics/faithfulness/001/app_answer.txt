In Cobb County, the blasting permit escrow requirements specify that prior to issuance of a blasting permit, the applicant may be required to deliver an executed escrow agreement acceptable to the county attorney's office, fully funded with cash in the amount of $50,000. This escrow agreement is part of the administrative process for the blasting permit (Local 2).

No additional details about conditions or use of the escrow funds were found in the provided documents. The requirement is clearly stated as a prerequisite for permit issuance but further specifics about the escrow agreement terms or release conditions are not included in the excerpts.

Sources: Cobb County Code of Ordinances, Sec. 18-360 and related sections (Local 2).
